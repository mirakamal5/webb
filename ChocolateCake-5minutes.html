5-minute stovetop chocolate cake

Nut-Free
Gluten-Free

Posted by: YassineCakesðŸŽ‚

Description:There are a lot of 5-minute cake-in-a-mug recipes out there, but I've taken the time to experiment with eight variations on a a recipe to come up with the best, tastiest, and most reliable 5-minute chocolate cake on the web. This quick and easy chocolate cake recipe will change your life! Now you can have a rich, chocolate dessert in an instant.

Prep Time: 1 min | Cook Time: 3-4 min | Servings: 8 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 50g white sugar
  • 12g unsweetened cocao powder
  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 0.75g salt (a tiny pinch)
  • 45g milk
  • 28g canola oil (or any neutral oil)
  • 15g water
  • 1g vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil (for greasing the pan)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  2. Pour in the milk, oil, water, and vanilla extract. Stir until the batter is smooth and no lumps remain.
  3. Grease a small non-stick pan (about 6-8 inches) with butter or oil and heat it over low-medium heat.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and spread it evenly. Cover the pan with a lid and let it cook on low heat. Check if the top is set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. If you want a more even cook, carefully flip the cake and cook for an additional minute on the other side.
  6. Slide the cake onto a plate, let it cool slightly, and enjoy! Add toppings like powdered sugar, chocolate sauce, or fresh fruit if desired.
