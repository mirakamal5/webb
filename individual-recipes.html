Description: A delightful and easy-to-make dessert perfect for any occasion.

Prep Time: 15 min | Cook Time: 30 min | Servings: 8 | Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g Flour
  • 100g Sugar
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients together.
  2. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  3. Let it cool and enjoy!
