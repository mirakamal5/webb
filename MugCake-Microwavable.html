Mug Cake

Vegetarian
Nut-Free

Posted by: MiraCooksüç∞

Description: This chocolate mug cake is made in the microwave for a fudgy, chocolaty treat that is truly decadent. It's a great recipe for nights when I need a yummy dessert that's ready in less than 10 minutes! Add a few chocolate chips to make it extra rich and gooey.

Prep Time: 5 min | Cook Time: 2 min | Servings: 1 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 50g white sugar
  • 12g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 0.5g baking soda
  • 45g milk
  • 0.75g salt
  • 28g canola oil
  • 15g water
  • 1g vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.
  2. Cook in the microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
